# CRSS — 90-Day GTM Plan (ADHD-friendly)

> **Context:** You are currently unemployed and not affiliated with any organization. This plan is written for a solo founder with full-time availability and no institutional backing. The credibility pitch is personal expertise in EU regulatory law and graph-AI, not lab affiliation. Funding routes requiring university IP or employment are flagged as blocked or requiring a partner.

> **Product:** CRSS (Compliance Readiness Support System) — a GraphRAG-powered AI agent that gives instant, citation-grounded answers to complex EU regulatory compliance questions across MDR 2017/745, IVDR 2017/746, EU AI Act 2024/1689, and MDCG guidance. Working demo exists.

> Half-day tasks. Verb + object + done-criterion. Import into Notion / Todoist / Things / TickTick / Obsidian.

## How to use this file (3 rules)

1. **Only look at the current week.** Collapse everything else. The plan is 12 weeks; today is one week.
2. **Pick One Big Thing (OBT) per week.** If only the OBT gets done, the week was a win.
3. **If stuck, do the "If stuck" micro-task.** 15 minutes, no thinking required. Streak > intensity.

Energy tags: `#deep` (focused work), `#admin` (low energy), `#social` (calls/meetings/email), `#writing`.
